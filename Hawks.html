Hawks Facts

When the Blackhawks won the Stanley Cup in 1938 they did it with a regular season record of 11-25. Almost missing the playoffs altogether.

The Blackhawks were the first NHL team to average more than 20,000 fans per game attendance in 1994, when they averaged 20,818 in the brand new United Center.

The original team logo was designed by Irene Castle. Irene was the wife of the team's first owner and coffee tycoon, Frederic McLaughlin.
